THE AWARDING

How honors are conferred

Each evening, the Keeper reads the Logbook and the ledgers. When a camper earns her patch, she is named in the next morning's letter and applauded in the Lodge thread — by name, before all assembled. The final honor, the Pontoon, is conferred in person, on the water, at the start of her private engagement.
